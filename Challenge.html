Disney World

Disney World is an amusement park located in Orlando, Florida. It is widely regarded as the most magical park in the world!

Epcot Center

Epcot is a theme park at Walt Disney World Resort featuring exciting attractions, international pavilions, award-winning fireworks and seasonal special events. It is open from 9:00am to 9:00pm all week. I plan to go there at 08:30am to beat the crowds.
